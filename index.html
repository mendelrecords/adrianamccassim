Asheville-born, LA-based. A garage folk musician, drawing from her DIY roots and Southern music traditions. McCassim’s work balances grit with emotional clarity.

Her 2025 debut album, See It Fades introduced her raw lyricism and darkly textured sound to wide acclaim. Recorded with producer Ryan Pollie (Tyler Ballgame) and mixed by Alex Farrar (Angel Olsen, Wednesday), the project showcased her ability to turn heartbreak into something timeless.
